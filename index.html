Biography

I am a biologist who likes to code!

I am studying B.Tech.-M.Tech. Dual Degree in Bioengineering at IIT Mandi. My areas of interest are Synthetic Biology, Computational Biology, Astrobiology, Evolutionary Sciences and Data Science. I have done quite a few projects the fields mentioned above. I started my B.Tech. here at IIT Mandi in 2019. In my third year of dual degree program, I chose Computational Bioengineering as my specialization. I also have experience in Team Leading and Volunteering. I had been Leader Coordinator at Space Technology and Astronomy Cell, IIT Mandi. I am also lead founder of Kamand Bioengineering Group here at IIT Mandi.

Industrial Internship
Wrig Nanosystems, Dec 2021 – Feb 2022, 2 months

I was offered a Project Management Internship at Wrig Nanosystems, a New Delhi based startup making Biomedical devices in Parwanoo, Himachal Pradesh. The internship was on-site in Parwanoo.
I joined the company in December 2021. Worked on major expansion projects both in production and R&D department. And in the end, successfully completed the internship in February 2022. During my internship, I got chance to work in-person directly with the Founder and Managing Director, Ambar Srivastava, of the company for a week.
One of the product of the company is a biomedical strip which is used to check the level of hemoglobin in blood. I worked on projects to improve the quality assurance process of the product. I worked on development of algorithm for Batch code (quality parameter) automation, preparation of a new algorithm for the quality testing of TrueHb strips, I worked on designing IoT network to improve the quality assurance process of the product as well.
